WHO IS BUGHA ?

Bugha is the first Fortnite player to win theFortnite World Cup and pocket himself a cool $3,000,000 in prize money along the way. He was relatively unknown to the Fortnite Community before the big showdown event in New York City but is now a famous face amongst Fortnite fans. Bugha put on an impressive showing, beating out the rest of the competitive player pool by a wide margin, including players like Tfue. Bugha has his own YouTube channel and regularly streams competitive Fortnite gameplay on Twitch too. He is signed to the competitive gaming team SEN.
